Core Services

3D Plant Design and Modeling

  • Design and develop accurate 3D models of industrial plants, including piping, equipment, and structural components.
  • Create high-quality, detailed visualizations for both design and operational phases.
  • Integration of engineering data into 3D models for a seamless workflow.

Virtual Plant Simulation

  • Simulate plant processes to optimize workflows and identify potential bottlenecks before actual construction.
  • Analyze the impact of design changes through real-time simulations.

BIM Integration

  • Integration of 3D models with BIM to streamline the planning, design, and construction process.
  • Facilitate collaborative workflows among engineers, architects, and contractors.

As-Built Modeling

  • Create accurate as-built 3D models of existing plants for retrofits, maintenance, or expansion.
  • Provide precise data for operations and maintenance teams.

Process Design and Engineering Solutions

  • Engineering analysis and design for efficient plant processes.
  • Development of piping and instrumentation diagrams (P&ID), heat balance calculations, and flow simulations.

Facility Management Solutions

  • Using 3D models to assist with plant maintenance, asset management, and facility lifecycle management.
