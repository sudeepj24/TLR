About us

'The Lotus Roots' is a specialized provider of PDM and PLM services, helping businesses streamline their product development processes, improve collaboration, and enhance data management efficiency. Our solutions empower clients to manage their product data across the entire lifecycle, from concept to production, using advanced Autodesk tools like Vault and Fusion 360 Manage. With a team of experienced consultants and engineers, we provide tailored PDM and PLM implementations that address unique business challenges.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with cutting-edge CAD solutions and expertise in Autodesk software, ensuring precision, efficiency, and innovation in every project.

AutoCAD Design and Drafting

3ds Max Visualization

PDM PLM Services
